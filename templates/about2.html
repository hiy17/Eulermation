About Eulermation

Making Euler circuits interactive, visual, and fun to learn.

Eulermation is a web application designed to help users understand and visualize Euler circuits in graphs. We focus specifically on finding and illustrating Euler circuits ‚Äî paths that visit every edge exactly once and return to the starting point.

With Eulermation, users can create custom graphs (up to 10 vertices), input edge connections manually, and watch the system find an Euler circuit, step-by-step.

Our goal is to make the abstract concept of Euler circuits clear, interactive, and engaging for learners, educators, and anyone curious about graph theory.

Our Mission

We aim to bridge the gap between theory and application by making complex topics in graph theory accessible to everyone ‚Äî one circuit at a time.
