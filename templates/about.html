About Eulermation

Eulermation is a web application designed to help users understand and visualize Euler circuits in graphs. We focus specifically on finding and illustrating Euler circuits — paths that visit every edge exactly once and return to the starting point.
With Eulermation, users can create custom graphs (up to 10 vertices), input edge connections manually, and watch the system find an Euler circuit, step-by-step.
Our goal is to make the abstract concept of Euler circuits clear, interactive, and engaging for learners, educators, and anyone curious about graph theory.
